[Generation Rent](https://www.generationrent.org/) campaigns for safe, fair and secure homes for renters.

We built a website to support their [National Renters Manifesto](https://www.rentermanifesto.org/read_the_manifesto_full) campaign during the 2019 General Election. The tool allows people to explore what the renting crisis looks like in their area, add their own experiences of private renting and sign the manifesto.
