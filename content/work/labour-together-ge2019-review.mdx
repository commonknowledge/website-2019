Common Knowledge interviewed a range of Labour Party staff, elected representatives and activist members as a contribution to the [Labour Together 2019 election review](https://electionreview.labourtogether.uk), and contributed to editorial discussions between April and June.

Our research is [documented in a two subsidiary reports that were used to inform the main publication](/writing/labour-together) released on 19th June. They analyse the Labour Party's organisational structures, ground operations and digital technology.
